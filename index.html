About Us..

We are the Students' Gymkhana body that oversees student-alumni Relations, publicizing achievements of IIT Kanpur, and outreach programs in collaboration with the Dean of Resources and Alumni (DoRA), IIT Kanpur.

We organize talks and networking programs for mentorship and career guidance for the students.

We promote and encourage the strengthening and development of our student-alumni relations by organising a plethora of events, like Tips from the Top series, the Alumni Buddy program, Mock-en-Joy, Legacy Hunt. We also help in the organisation of Alumni Reunions, Chapter Meetups, and Convocation in collaboration with IITKAA and DORA. Apart from tirelessly working to create a harmonious student-alumni community, we raise funds for various movements like 'Fundraising for IITK Workers' and '#OneAlumnusOneStudent Fundraising Camp.'
